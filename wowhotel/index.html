ABOUT US

Convenience and comfort are what WOW Budget Cubao can offer. Located in the heart of Cubao, Quezon City, the hotel is a short walking distance to Gateway Mall, Ali Mall, SM Cubao, Farmers Plaza, and Araneta Coliseum. The hotel is near provincial bus terminals, perfect for the on-the-go travelers or even those visiting for business. WOW, Budget Hotel Cubao boasts of spacious One Hundred (100) fully-airconditioned guest rooms with free in-room wi-fi, Cable TV, a private toilet and bath equipped with a hot and cold shower. WOW Budget Hotel Cubao is indeed a great place to stay with your event, friends or loved ones.
